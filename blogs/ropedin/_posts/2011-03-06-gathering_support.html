February has been a month of building support, and preparing to build a community of prayers around us that will support us as we leave our comfortable community at St Paul's and step out into a culture that will present many challenges to us. To that end, we are announcing a series of events in which you can show your support.

Prayer Breakfast

Sunday 20th March 8:30-9:30am

Join us at our place for a light breakfast as we pray for Germany, Berlin, the church plant, and the part we will take in it all.

Address: 14/18-20 Doomben Ave, Eastwood

German Gebetsnacht

Wednesday 13th April 7:30-9:30pm

This will be a night where we'll give information about what we are doing, why we are going, and what we hope to achieve in Berlin. We'll spend a good amount of time in prayer, and there will be plenty of time for questions and discussion.

Address: St Paul's Foyer, 421 Old Northern Rd, Castle Hill

Prayer Breakfast

Sunday 29th May 8:30-9:30am

Join us at our place for a light breakfast as we pray for Germany, Berlin, the church plant, and the part we will take in it all.

Address: 14/18-20 Doomben Ave, Eastwood

Pretzel und Strudel Nacht

Wednesday 15th June 7:30-9:30pm

Salted pretzels and sticky apple desserts tickle your fancy? This is the night for you! No? Come support us anyway, this will be our last prayer meeting before we head to Germany.

Address: St Paul's Foyer, 421 Old Northern Rd, Castle Hill
